Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

2. How we use cookies

BimEnough.com uses various types of cookies and similar technologies like localStorage to enhance your browsing experience.

3. Types of cookies we use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function:

  • Language preferences: Remember your selected language (IT/EN)
  • Cookie consent: Remember your cookie preferences

Legal basis: Legitimate interest

3.2 LocalStorage (Local Storage)

We use browser localStorage for:

  • bimeCounters: Download and donation counters (statistics only)
  • bimeFollowedLinkedin: LinkedIn follow status for downloads
  • bimeFollowedYoutube: YouTube follow status for downloads
  • bimeCookieConsent: Cookie consent preferences

Legal basis: Consent and legitimate interest

3.3 Third-Party Cookies

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to improve the site's appearance. Google may set cookies for this service.

  • Purpose: Provide web fonts
  • Duration: Variable
  • Information: Google Privacy Policy

PayPal

For donations, we redirect to PayPal which may set its own cookies.

  • Purpose: Payment processing
  • Duration: According to PayPal policy
  • Information: PayPal Privacy Policy

4. Cookie duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when browser is closed
  • Persistent cookies: Remain until expiration or manual deletion
  • LocalStorage: Persists until manual deletion

5. How to manage cookies

5.1 Browser management

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

5.2 LocalStorage management

To clear localStorage data specific to this site:

  • Use the "Clear Local Data" button in our Privacy Policy
  • Use browser developer tools (F12) > Application/Storage > LocalStorage

6. Cookie consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. You can change your preferences at any time.

7. Policy updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
