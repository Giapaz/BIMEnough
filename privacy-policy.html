Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

1. Data Controller

BimEnough
Email: privacy@BimEnough.com
Support email: support@BimEnough.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage counters: We collect anonymous statistics on downloads and donations to improve our service
  • Site preferences: Selected language and social media status (LinkedIn/YouTube) for download functionality
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system for security and analysis purposes

2.2 Voluntarily Provided Data

  • Contact email: When you send us an email for support
  • Donation information: Handled directly by PayPal (see third-party services section)

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Site functionality: Provide requested features (downloads, language preferences)
  • Anonymous statistics: Monitor site usage for improvements
  • Technical support: Respond to your assistance requests
  • Security: Protect the site from abuse and attacks

4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on:

  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and statistics
  • Legitimate interest: For site security and essential functionality
  • Service execution: To provide requested downloads and support

5. Local Storage (LocalStorage)

Our site uses browser localStorage technology for:

  • Statistical counters: Store download and donation counters locally
  • Social gate: Remember social media follow status
  • Preferences: Selected language and other site preferences

Data control: You can delete this data at any time from your browser settings or using the "Clear Local Data" button at the bottom of this page.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to improve the site's appearance. Google may collect data for this functionality. Google Privacy Policy

6.2 PayPal

Donations are handled by PayPal. We do not have access to your payment data. PayPal Privacy Policy

6.3 Social Media

Links to our social profiles (LinkedIn, YouTube) may involve tracking by these platforms:

  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy
  • YouTube/Google Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

Data is retained for the time necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • LocalStorage data: Until manual or browser deletion
  • Support emails: Up to 2 years from request resolution
  • Security logs: Up to 1 year

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Obtain information about the data we process
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Restriction: Limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@BimEnough.com

9. Security

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. International Transfers

Some third-party services (Google Fonts, PayPal) may transfer data outside the EU. These transfers are made with adequate safeguards for data protection.

11. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Substantial changes will be communicated through a notice on the site.

12. Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority for data protection in your country.

13. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy:

  • Privacy email: privacy@BimEnough.com
  • Support email: support@BimEnough.com
