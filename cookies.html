Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience. This Cookie Policy explains how BimEnough uses cookies on our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Essential Cookies We Use:

  • Cookie Consent: Remembers your cookie preferences and consent choices
  • Language Preference: Stores your selected language (English/Italian)
  • Security: Basic security measures to protect against malicious activity

Cookies We Don't Use

We are committed to minimal data collection. We do not use:

  • Analytics or tracking cookies
  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Third-party tracking technologies

Purpose of Our Cookies

Language Preferences

We store your language preference (English or Italian) so you don't have to select it every time you visit our website. This improves your browsing experience by automatically displaying content in your preferred language.

Cookie Consent Management

We remember your cookie consent choices so we don't have to ask you again on every visit. This cookie itself is essential for managing your privacy preferences.

Website Functionality

Some cookies are necessary for basic website functionality, such as maintaining secure connections and ensuring the website loads correctly across different devices.

Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

Some cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. These are called session cookies and are used for basic website functionality during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

Other cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. These persistent cookies remember your preferences between visits. Our persistent cookies typically last for 1 year.

Your Cookie Choices

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept our essential cookies or adjust your preferences. You can change your mind at any time by accessing the cookie settings.

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block future cookies from being set
  • Get notified when cookies are being set

Impact of Blocking Cookies

If you choose to block or delete our essential cookies, some parts of our website may not work properly. For example, you may need to select your language preference on each visit, and the website may not remember your cookie consent choices.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party cookies on our website. All cookies are set directly by our website and are under our control. We do not allow external services to track your activity on our site through cookies.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
